The Elevator Pitch For the lazy and impatient.

ngbp-slim-sass is a fork of ngBoilerplate the opinionated kickstarter for web development projects. It's an attempt to create a slimed down simple starter for new web sites and apps: just download it and start coding. The goal is to provide just what you need you need to get started, and leave room for you to add the frameworks and libraries that you want use. It has a best practice directory structure to ensure maximum code reuse. And it's all tied together with a robust build system chock-full of some time-saving goodness.

Why?

Because I make web apps, and I like to try out different frameworks and combinations of libraries. With AngularJS rocking the front-end development world I wanted to create a generic boilerplate that could tie in AngularJS with every framework that I wanted to try. ngbp-slim-sass attempts to reach that goal. Maybe you want to use Bootstrap for your next project...go ahead! Want to give the Foundation Framework a go...why not? Maybe Susy Grids are your cup of tea... drink up!

The original ngBoilerplate is an awesome kickstarter for your project, but I found myself repeatedly removing components I didn't want, and switching my projects from LESS to SASS, so I decided it was high time I went ahead and created my own fork.

So What's Included? Just the minimum

This slim down version of ngBoilerplate makes use of the SASS CSS preprocessor for all of your awesome styling needs. The Font Awesome, collection of font-based icons is hanging around to fill that icon shaped hole in your heart. Most important of all: the almighty power of AngularJS is running this show with its super neato client side superpowered core.

What's Next? Whatever you want
